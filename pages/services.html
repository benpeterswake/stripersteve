Guide Services

Whether this is your first time fishing or you are a seasoned angler, we can show you how to find and catch Stripers. My trips are very flexible, from a half-day starting in the morning or afternoon (4 hours) to full days (8 hours). I provide instruction as needed on how to use the fishing tackle, if some folks are new to the game. I will supply fishing gear, bait, ice, water, and soft drinks. Things you should bring:

  • Dress appropriately for the weather.
  • Sunglasses and a hat.
  • Sunblock
  • Any food or snacks you want for the boat.
